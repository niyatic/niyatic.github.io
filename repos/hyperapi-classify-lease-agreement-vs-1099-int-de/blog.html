A lease agreement misrouted as a Form 1099-INT corrupts downstream posting

AP/AR routing depends on the document type; a lease agreement booked on the Form 1099-INT workflow posts to the wrong sub-ledger. Gating on the recognised two-class set catches off-taxonomy scans before they reach the GL. hyperapi-classify-lease-agreement-vs-1099-int-de turns the manual glance into one call that returns the extracted fields plus a deterministic verdict — the pass/fail gate is computed locally, not by the model, so it is reproducible and audit-friendly.

The HyperAPI classify primitive does the OCR, layout, and field work server-side on an open-source model fine-tuned by Hyperbots (Hyperbots IP). A low-confidence first pass is automatically re-run on a hi-res profile — there is no review queue and no person in the path; documents that stay weak come back flagged for the caller's automation to route.

Accuracy figures are published at apis.hyperbots.com and are not restated here.
