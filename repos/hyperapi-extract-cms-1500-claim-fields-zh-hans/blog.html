Billing NPI, diagnosis and charge fields lifted off a a CMS-1500 professional claim form must be present before the record can post

Downstream automation keys on the a CMS-1500 professional claim form billing_npi, diagnosis_code, total_charge; a blank or malformed value silently breaks matching and posting. The local rule asserts the required fields are present and well-formed. hyperapi-extract-cms-1500-claim-fields-zh-hans turns the manual glance into one call that returns the extracted fields plus a deterministic verdict — the pass/fail gate is computed locally, not by the model, so it is reproducible and audit-friendly.

The HyperAPI extract primitive does the OCR, layout, and field work server-side on an open-source model fine-tuned by Hyperbots (Hyperbots IP). A low-confidence first pass is automatically re-run on a hi-res profile — there is no review queue and no person in the path; documents that stay weak come back flagged for the caller's automation to route.

Accuracy figures are published at apis.hyperbots.com and are not restated here.
