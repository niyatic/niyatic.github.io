Extract corporate-card key fields and grade extraction quality as an ordered band (clean..failed) with confidence and margin gates.

A corporate-card statement's totals feed expense reconciliation. This package is a typed client around the HyperAPI extract primitive returning the fields and an ordered quality grade plus the ranked distribution.

The hard part isn't reading the page

It's the boundary cases. A document sitting right between minor_gaps and clean reads 0.53 / 0.44 -- "confident" by a single number, genuinely ambiguous in fact. This package gates on confidence and the top-vs-runner-up margin, so the borderline case is caught and automatically re-extracted on a stronger profile.

Fails safe, never a queue

When the model genuinely cannot decide, the decision fails safe to failed -- grading an undecidable extraction 'failed' keeps unreliable totals out of automated expense reconciliation. Documents that stay weak come back trustworthy=false with the full ranking, for your automation to route by rule. It is never queued to a person.

Five lines

``python from hyperapi_extract_corporate_card_fields import extract_file r = extract_file("document.pdf") print(r.decision.label, r.trustworthy) ``

Accuracy is published at apis.hyperbots.com. The model is an open-source document model fine-tuned by Hyperbots (Hyperbots IP); this repo is the typed client around it.
