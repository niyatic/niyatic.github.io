📐 Our content strategy

Hyperbots content earns trust by being measurably right, not loud. Every page we ship traces back to anchored evidence — a benchmark we ran, a corpus we processed, a customer case with consent. Nothing invented; everything sourceable.

1. Anchored over assertive
Numbers are measured on disk this session or marked [PLACEHOLDER]. A pointer is not a claim. If we can't source it, we don't print it.
2. Depth bar from the di-study
14 dimensions, doc-type × failure-mode matrices, per-line precision/recall — the methodology spine for every deep study. No marketing-grade comparisons.
3. Own the loss
Where competitors win, we say so. The "narrow honest edge" play: credibility comes from disclosure, not omission.
4. Review chain + human gate
Tech / brand / legal review on every external piece. Customer-named or competitor-named content needs explicit sign-off before publish.
Anchor data, measured 2026-05-25: Azure DI prebuilt-invoice on the 10 longest invoices in dataset_95 (49–323 line items each) — line-count preservation mean 0.640, exact-match docs 1/10, line-item set-F1 mean 0.344. One 88-line invoice came back as 2,150 rows. This is the kind of evidence the strategy is built on.
