§0 — Honest framing

Researchers, not advocates. The framework is engineered to defensibly answer "is the HyperAPI parse primitive the best general parsing primitive across finance documents" — and to do so in a way that succeeds whether parse wins or loses on any given dimension. The bar is ParseBench (arXiv:2604.08538) / NeurIPS-paper, strictly higher than SAVIOR-Bench.

§6 — Methodology snapshot

Full doc: methodology.md. Highlights:

  • Bootstrap — 2000 resamples, seed 20260516; per-doc score vectors mandatory; aggregate-only cells ship literal n never inferred.
  • Paired bootstrap — D-36 lift + any paired comparison.
  • Multiplicity — Holm-Bonferroni on cross-dimension headline claims (family-wise α=0.05; per-dim α≈0.001).
  • Pin families — parse-deep (T=0.1, top_p=0.9, max_tokens=2048) · TR-000 (T=0, top_p=1, 4096) · extract (T=0, top_p=1, 4096). Cross-family cells (D-31, D-36) flagged apples-to-oranges.
  • NeurIPS reproducibility checklist — answered item-by-item in methodology §8.
