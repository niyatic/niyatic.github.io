QuickBooks Online vs Desktop: the SMB-to-mid-market upgrade path — plan

1. Headline + hook

Hook: "The SMB that started on QuickBooks Desktop and is moving to QuickBooks Online (or vice versa, more often than the marketing says) has the same problem as the SAP shop mid-cutover: two back-ends, one vendor list, one closing process. The connector framework's descriptor pattern spans both — and at this size, the agent compresses what used to be a weekend of accountant-driven file-export work into hours of configuration."

Honest framing: a QuickBooks Online descriptor is compiled on disk (connectors/erp/__pycache__/quickbooks.cpython-311.pyc). QuickBooks Desktop (QBD) is a descriptor-extension claim — same pattern, not built.
