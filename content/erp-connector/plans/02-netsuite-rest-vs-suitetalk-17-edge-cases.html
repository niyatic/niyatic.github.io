3.2 Depth — the 17 edge cases (PLACEHOLDER — audit required)

The plan names the categories up front; the specific 17 must be audit-derived from the netsuite descriptor + skill body. Categories:

  1. Subsidiary scoping — OneWorld multi-subsidiary; cross-subsidiary

refusal vs allowed.

  1. Internal-id collisions — vendor-id reused across subsidiaries.
  2. TBA token rotation — invalid_grant hard stop, not a silent retry.
  3. Vendor vs vendor-credit — transactionType distinction.
  4. Currency & subsidiary base currency — FX policy on the descriptor.
  5. Tax-code resolution — NetSuite's TaxCode list, per-subsidiary.
  6. Custom-segment fields — the cseg_* namespace; additive handling.
  7. Item vs expense lines — line-type distinction.
  8. Memo-vs-description truncation — the 999-char limit.
  9. Posting period closed — the period-locked error class.
  10. Approval routing state — approvalstatus field's enum.
  11. Reverse charge VAT — EU posting on a US-base subsidiary.
  12. PO-bill matching — three-way match plumbing.
  13. Concurrency limits — SuiteTalk RPS limit; the framework's RPM

budget aligns.

  1. Batch boundary — REST's request-size limit.
  2. Idempotency on retry — externalId deduplication.
  3. Soft-delete vs hard-delete — isinactive handling.

Each of the above will be either confirmed in the descriptor source or tagged [PLACEHOLDER — depth claim needs source].
