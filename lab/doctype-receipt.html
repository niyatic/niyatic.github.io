Receipt — OCR engines compared

Receipts and short-form documents are OCR-bound. This page reports the OCR edit-distance leaderboard (lower is better) across 31 OCR engines + SAVIOR-bench word-recall — the two metric families most predictive of receipt-extraction quality.

Doc-type: receipt · Task families: ocr · savior · Source: DC-000__ocr-edit-distance-v1__*.json + DC-000__savior-bench-v1__*.json

Honest framing

This is not a head-to-head accuracy claim.
  • OCR edit-distance values are attested — reported from paper or vendor disclosure on the OmniDocBench-shape protocol.
  • SAVIOR-bench word-recall (n=509) is attested, reported per the SAVIOR-Bench v1 protocol.
  • Receipt-specific extraction F1 is PENDING — the existing corpus is invoice + bank-statement weighted.
  • HyperAPI accuracy reported per apis.hyperbots.com; HyperAPI PaddleOCR OURS = the open-source PaddleOCR fine-tuned by Hyperbots.
